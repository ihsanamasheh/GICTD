GICTD Vision:

The vision of (GICTD), is a strategic vision based on providing an integrated series of consulting services for business development with a high level of quality for public, private & non-Governmental Organization (NGO) sectors, in order to contribute to economic, social and cultural development, and this is achieved through an appropriate environment that includes the development of thought with the support of specialized professional work teams.
(GICTD) vision includes:
- Contribute to providing a variety of professional consulting services in the field of business.
- Expanding knowledge and skills for individuals.
- Provide solutions to the current & future obstacles that can add competitive value in order to achieve the strategic plan target efficiently and effectively.

GICTD Mission:

The objective for (GICTD) is not only to resolve business obstacles, rather to turn them into valuable points.
(GICTD) Values: Providing professional services to our clients based on three main aspects:
1- Independency:
(GICTD) is a fully independent Co. that is not related to/nor is part of any local or international holding groups operating in the business domain.
2- Professionality:
The (GICTD) provides its services through specialized team featuring highly qualified academic efficiency, and well-trained professionals with real practical experience.
3- Knowledge and Tools:
The (GICTD) service delivery is based on the latest supporting tools. We are also in continuous cooperation with international professional institutions that contribute to the development of our capabilities and expertise.
